Requirements:
    python 3.13.5 or greater
    pygame (obtainable via pip or your preferred package manager)
    Speakers or Headphones
    Literally any video card (integrated is fine)
    Some RAM

How to Play:
    Shoot Arrow - Z
    Jump - X
    Run - Left Arrow / Right Arrow

On a Dark and Stormy night while watching "Lord of the Rings", 
    the cacocophonous boom of a thunderclap explodes above - startling
    you into a dizzy spell! You fall upon the floor striking it with your
    skull, the searing impact dampened as the already dark room fades to 
    black.

    You awake in a forest, storming much like the one outside your home.
    Dressed in leather armor and equipped with a Ranger's Bow, how many 
    Orcs can you fight through on your quest to make it home?!
